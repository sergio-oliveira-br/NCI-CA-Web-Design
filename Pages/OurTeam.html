Welcome to the amazing world of Joyful Zoo, where each employee is a unique piece of this enchanted puzzle!

Meet our lovable team members, each with their special personalities, ready to delight the young audience.

Meet Our Team

Bruno is the bold exploring bear of Joyful Zoo. Dressed in an explorer vest and carrying a backpack full of wonders, he loves taking children on exciting expeditions through the zoo. With his curly mustache and a map in his paw, Bruno is the embodiment of courage and discovery.

Ricardo is a very busy business bunny! Always dressed in a sharp suit and carrying a folder full of carrots (and reports), he is the zoo's executive rabbit. With his charm and problem-solving skills, Ricardo ensures that all zoo operations are running smoothly.

These fearless ducklings are the cutest police officers in town! With their impeccably aligned feathers and shiny badges, Patrol Officers Penelope and Pablo ensure that all zoo animals are safe and happy. With their fun whistles and flashy wings, they are the fearless guardians of the animal kingdom.

Dr. Mia is the most loving medical kitten you've ever seen! With her first aid kit and a cute stethoscope around her neck, she takes care of all the zoo animals. Whether it's a hurt paw or a sniffly nose, Dr. Mia is always ready to heal with her gentle, purring touch.

Bolt is the most adventurous puppy in Joyful Zoo! Always with his tongue out and a bandana in the wind, he's ready for any challenge. Whether climbing trees, digging holes, or just spreading joy, Bolt is the perfect companion for a fun afternoon at the zoo.

Together, these incredible employees make Joyful Zoo a magical place, full of laughter, adventures, and learning for all the children who visit!
